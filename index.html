Our Story

Kerala Roots.
London Refinement.

ChaiLink was born from a single conviction - that a perfectly brewed cup of chai holds the power to slow a city down. Rooted in the cardamom-laced culinary traditions of Malabar and the backwaters of Kerala, we translate those heirloom recipes into a dining experience made for London's most discerning palates.

Every dish is a study in restraint and depth: slow-cooked, thoughtfully sourced, and presented with care. From our Neypathiri with aged beef roast to our single-origin filter kaapi, ChaiLink is a love letter to South India — plated for the capital.
