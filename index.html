Our Story

Kerala Roots.
London Refinement.

ChaiLink was born from a single conviction — that a perfectly brewed cup of chai holds the power to slow a city down. Rooted in the cardamom-laced culinary traditions of Malabar and the backwaters of Kerala, we translate those heirloom recipes into a dining experience made for London's most discerning palates.

Every dish is a study in restraint and depth: slow-cooked, thoughtfully sourced, and presented with care. From our Neypathiri with aged beef roast to our single-origin filter kaapi, ChaiLink is a love letter to South India — plated for the capital.

Chef's Table

Regional Signatures

Dishes rooted in the Palakkad Gap's singular culinary crossroads — where Tamil Brahmin tradition meets Malabar Muslim heritage. Available on selected evenings.

🏆 Bestseller

Mandi Platter

Aromatic Mandi rice with your choice of protein — a spread to remember, for tables that love to share. Serves 3–4.

🌿 Chef's Pick

Fish Pollichathu

Whole fish marinated in Malabar spices, wrapped in banana leaf and slow-cooked to perfection. A true Kerala classic.

⭐ Chai Link Special

Pathiri Beef

Thin steamed rice flour flatbreads paired with deeply spiced Malabar beef — an iconic Malappuram combination.
